Steak Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Ribeye steak
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Fresh rosemary

Instructions:

  1. Season the steak generously with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat a skillet over high heat and add butter, garlic, and rosemary.
  3. Sear the steak on each side until it reaches your desired doneness.
  4. Baste the steak with the melted butter and herbs while cooking.
  5. Let the steak rest for a few minutes before serving.
