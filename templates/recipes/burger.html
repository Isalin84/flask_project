Burger Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Beef patty
  • Burger buns
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Instructions:

  1. Grill the beef patty until cooked to your preference.
  2. Toast the burger buns on the grill.
  3. Assemble the burger with cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles.
  4. Add ketchup and mustard to taste.
  5. Serve with fries or your favorite side.
