Cake Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Baking powder
  • Milk
  • Your favorite frosting

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
  4. Combine flour and baking powder, add to the creamed mixture, and mix well.
  5. Stir in the milk until the batter is smooth.
  6. Pour batter into a greased cake pan and bake for 30-40 minutes.
  7. Let the cake cool, then frost with your favorite frosting.
